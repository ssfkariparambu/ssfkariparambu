Cerebrating Values

In Vaikom Muhammed Basheer's novel Ormayude Arakal, the scene where Basheer, who returns home after a long time, is invited to eat by his mother is presented in a very touching way. Basheer, who has traveled around the world for many years and finally reached his own home, is welcomed by his mother as if it were an ordinary day. There are no dramas or celebrations. The fact that his son has traveled all over the world is not an issue for his mother. For his mother, Basheer will always be her only son. Basheer describes this scene by saying that he has understood one of the greatest truths in his life.
